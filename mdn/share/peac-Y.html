“Why am I always like this?”

Have you ever found yourself pausing on that thought, late at night? Feeling worn out from fitting in with others, or carrying a personality you can never quite put into words — far more people live with this than you might imagine.

But there is nothing wrong with you. Perhaps you simply haven’t yet met a “mirror” that truly reflects who you are. The guardian bird reading is one such mirror.

What is the Guardian Bird Reading?

Using your birth date and your name, this reading reveals the one bird that watches over you. There are twenty guardian birds in all, each with its own character and its own gifts. There may come a moment when you realize that what you thought was a burden was, in fact, the shape of the wings you were born with.

What you will discover

  • Your true character and your hidden strengths
  • How you approach love, and who suits you well
  • The situations where you shine at work
  • Your relationship with money, and the flow of your fortune

Why a bird?

Our own flaws can be hard to accept. Yet when we can think, “that’s simply how this bird is,” the tension somehow eases from our shoulders. Through the image of a bird, you can look at yourself again with gentler eyes.

About your name

This reading uses your name as well as your birth date to calculate your destiny number. A nickname you are usually called by is perfectly fine — what matters most is knowing you as you naturally are. The bird revealed by a nickname is the one accompanying you as you are right now.

If, on the other hand, you wish to know the “true guardian bird” that has been with you since birth, try your full given name. Neither answer is more correct than the other — choose according to the self you wish to know.
